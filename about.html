🌍 About WanderMap

Hi, I'm Manik Rao, a passionate traveler, explorer, and web developer from India. I founded WanderMap to help travelers easily explore, plan, and navigate their journeys with accurate travel routes, real-time durations for multiple transport modes, and destination weather, all powered by Google Maps API.

🚀 Why I Built WanderMap

I’ve always loved traveling, but managing different apps for directions, weather, and destination guides was exhausting. I built WanderMap to combine them into a single clean, intuitive platform for travel enthusiasts like you.

🧭 What WanderMap Offers

  • 📍 Distance & travel time for Car, Walk, Cycle, Bus, Train
  • 🌦️ Real-time destination weather
  • 🗺️ Interactive Google Map routing
  • 🎒 Curated destination galleries and travel blogs
