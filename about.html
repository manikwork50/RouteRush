Hi, I'm Manik Rao, a passionate traveler and digital creator. I founded WanderMap to help fellow travel enthusiasts discover secret routes, plan smarter journeys, and explore hidden destinations across India and beyond.

I love road trips, biking to lesser-known places, and documenting my adventures. This website is a reflection of my journeys and built as a free tool for anyone who loves traveling like me.

WanderMap is powered by reliable, free, open-source services including OpenStreetMap for maps, OpenWeather API for live weather info, and OSRM for routing directions by car, cycle, or on foot.

You'll also find interactive maps, travel itineraries, weather updates, gallery pages, and regular travel blogs to keep your wanderlust alive!
