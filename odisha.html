Jaipur

History: Founded in 1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II.

Famous for: Amber Fort, City Palace, Hawa Mahal, gem markets.

Food: Dal Baati Churma, Laal Maas.

Craft: Blue Pottery, Bandhej textiles.

Nearest Airport: Jaipur Airport (12 km)

Nearest Railway Station: Jaipur Railway Station (5 km)

Nearest Bus Stand: Sindhi Camp Bus Stand (3 km)
